List of Pokémon in Pokémon Legends: Z-A

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.
Jump to navigationJump to search

This is a list of Pokémon available in Pokémon Legends: Z-A. 364 Pokémon are available in the game; 232 were present in the game at the time of release while 132 were introduced in version 2.0.0 with the release of the Mega Dimension DLC. All Pokémon are present in either the Lumiose Pokédex or the Hyperspace Pokédex. Mega Evolution appears in Pokémon Legends: Z-A, with all of the Mega Evolutions introduced in Pokémon X and Y and Pokémon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire returning, in addition to 48 new Mega-Evolved forms introduced in this game.

Regional forms are listed alongside the original forms of their respective species, while Mega Evolutions, as well as other Pokémon forms, are listed on separate sections below. A Pokémon is considered "normally available" if it can be obtained without the use of events or transferring from another game.
