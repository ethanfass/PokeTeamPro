This is a list of Pokťmon in the order dictated by the Crown Tundra regional Pokťdex.

The Crown Tundra Pokťdex is one of three regional Pokťdexes featured in Pokťmon Sword and Shield, along with the Galar Pokťdex and the Isle of Armor Pokťdex. It was added to the game in The Crown Tundra expansion and contains 210 Pokťmon. The Crown Tundra Pokťdex also appears as one of the regional Pokťdexes in the mobile version of Pokťmon HOME, where it can only be filled with Pokťmon that originate from Sword and Shield.

The 210 Pokťmon in the Pokťdex follow this breakdown:

  • 74 are exclusive to the Crown Tundra (the 5 new and 66 returning Pokťmon added in Version 1.3.0, plus the three Swords of Justice, not counting the returning Pokťmon added that are not included in the Pokťdex).
  • 136 also appear in other areas in Galar.

Only Pokťmon in the Galar, Isle of Armor, and Crown Tundra Pokťdexes, as well as a select few outside of the aforementioned Pokťdexes, can be transferred from Pokťmon HOME to Pokťmon Sword and Shield.

Although the in-game Pokťdex list shows the Pokťmon with the numbers listed below, the individual entries for each Pokťmon instead display their Galar Pokťdex numbers, or no number at all for Pokťmon not in the Galar Pokťdex.

Trivia

  • The Crown Tundra Pokťdex lists Umbreon before Espeon and Glaceon before Leafeon, despite the fact that the reverse is true in all other Pokťdex listings these Pokťmon appear in (including the Galar Pokťdex).
  • Druddigon is the only Pokťmon to appear in both the Isle of Armor Pokťdex and the Crown Tundra Pokťdex but not the main Galar Pokťdex.
  • Despite being found as an overworld spawn in the Frigid Sea, Wailmer is not in the Crown Tundra Pokťdex. It is the only Pokťmon that can be found through random encounters in the Crown Tundra to not appear in this Pokťdex.
