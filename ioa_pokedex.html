This is a list of Pokťmon in the order dictated by the Isle of Armor regional Pokťdex.

The Isle of Armor Pokťdex is one of three regional Pokťdexes featured in Pokťmon Sword and Shield, along with the Galar Pokťdex and the Crown Tundra Pokťdex. It was added to the game in The Isle of Armor expansion and contains 211 Pokťmon, though only 210 are required to be obtained to complete the Pokťdex (Zarude is not required). The Isle of Armor Pokťdex also appears as one of the regional Pokťdexes in the mobile version of Pokťmon HOME, where it can only be filled with Pokťmon that originate from Sword and Shield; this version of the Pokťdex has only 210 Pokťmon, as Zarude is excluded.

The 211 Pokťmon in the Pokťdex follow this breakdown:

  • 109 are exclusive to the Isle of Armor (the 3 new and 102 returning Pokťmon added in Version 1.2.0, plus the two Kanto first partner Pokťmon lines, minus Druddigon and Magearna).
  • 101 also appear in the main region of Galar.
  • 1 also later appears in the Crown Tundra (Druddigon).

Only Pokťmon in the Galar, Isle of Armor, and Crown Tundra Pokťdexes, as well as a select few outside of the aforementioned Pokťdexes, can be transferred from Pokťmon HOME to Pokťmon Sword and Shield.

Although the in-game Pokťdex list shows the Pokťmon with the numbers listed below, the individual entries for each Pokťmon instead display their Galar Pokťdex numbers, or no number at all for Pokťmon not in the Galar Pokťdex.

Trivia

  • The Isle of Armor Pokťdex lists Shelmet and Accelgor before Karrablast and Escavalier, despite the fact that the reverse is true in all other Pokťdex listings these Pokťmon appear in (including the Galar Pokťdex).
  • Dhelmise does not appear in this Pokťdex despite being a fishing encounter at Challenge Beach.
  • Rotom does not appear in this Pokťdex despite being a wanderer encounter at the Workout Sea.
  • Slowking's Galarian form, which can be registered in the Isle of Armor Pokťdex, was not present in the game until the release of The Crown Tundra expansion.
  • The Isle of Armor Pokťdex is the only Pokťdex in Pokťmon Sword and Shield with Pokťmon that can be registered but are not required for completion (namely Zarude).
