How to Get Mega Darkrai in Pokemon Legends: Z-A Mega Dimension DLC

Defeat Rogue Mega Darkrai

You can acquire the Darkranite by defeating a Rogue Mega Darkrai.

Rogue Mega Pokemon are wild Pokemon that Mega Evolve without a trainer and are much stronger than regular Wild Pokemon. However, you do not have to face them alone as you will often be accompanied by an ally from Team MZ when taking them down.

Mega Darkrai Best Nature and Moveset

Modest or Timid

Nature Stat Changes
Modest ? SpAtk, ? Attack
Timid ? Speed, ? Attack

Since Darkrai is a Fast Special Attacker, it's best Nature is either Modest or Timid to boost its Special Attack or Speed further, while reducing its Attack stat.

Alternative Natures

Nature Stat Changes
Mild ? SpAtk, ? Defense
Rash ? Speed, ? SpDef

You can also give Darkrai either Mild or Rash nature, if you will mainly use it for Story Content.

Keep in mind that giving Darkrai any of the above two natures will increase the damage it receives, making it less ideal for Competitive Play and harder post-game content.

Use Mints to Change Natures

If your Darkrai has a different Nature than the ones we recommend above, you can use a Mint to change its Nature.
