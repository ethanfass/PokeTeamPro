How to Get Shiny Pokemon

Make Donuts with High Sweet Flavor

In the Mega Dimension DLC, you can make Donuts with the Sparkling Power effect, which raises the chance of encountering Shiny Pokemon in Hyperspace Wild Zones. This is useful to catch Shiny forms of Pokemon that appear in the DLC.

How to Make Shiny Donut Recipe

Repeatedly Reset Pokemon Spawn

Repeatedly resetting Pokemon spawns in an area can reroll the chances of spawning a Shiny. This can be achieved by using fast travel, resting, or changing the time on benches.

A Shiny Pokemon does not despawn once it appears. Players may save the game before attempting to catch the Shiny and save scum when failing the capture.

Shiny Hunting Guide

Complete Side Mission 17

A Shiny Mareep can be captured after completing Side Mission 17. This Side Mission is available after completing Main Mission 08.

A Shiny Mareep? Walkthrough
